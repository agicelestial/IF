Donate to support the Illustrative Fortran project.

Your donations to support the Illustrative Fortran project will be highly appreciated. The project is currently not sponsored by any establishment, firm, company, or cooperate body. The expenses, maintenance and all are taken care of by one person—Agi Celestial. It is a measure of burden added to the burdens that its author and developer bears. Your donations, regardless of how small, will help to push the project further and quicken its growth by contributing to the taking care of some or all expenses incurred from it.

Your opinions and comments are highly appreciated as well. In contrast to making monetary donations, one can support the Illustrative Fortran project by emailing the author and developer on important matters about the project. Like its impact and ways it requires improvement, features not required or required, errors, things or parts that are not clear either presented in words or as images, and the rest. Another way of doing this is by taking the Illustrative Fortran survey, if you can, at the homepage of the official website. Such information are very important to the author and developer of Illustrative Fortran. And they give insight to what is required and what should be done to improve the project.

Donation to support Illustrative Fortran can be through any means that one prefers. It can be money donation, gift cards, cryptos, promotion codes, and others. Send gift cards and promotion codes to the same email, or alternatively agigozsoft@gmail.com. For your convenience, if there happens to be a means you prefer for supporting Illustrative Fortran but there is neither no provided option for such nor a way to go about the support reaching the author and developer of the project, please, forward such an information to any of the emails provided here and arrangement will be made to get your donation or support through your preferred means.
